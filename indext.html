WEDDING INFORMATION

Make It Your Own

Your experience is our top priority, which is why we’re thrilled to offer new optional extras. As part of our dedication to wedding customers, we now provide the opportunity to book additional afternoon sets, ideal for cocktail hour or entertaining your guests while you’re off having your photos taken! For a small charge we can also learn a specific first dance song, unique to you! (Subject to the suitability of the song of course!)

Unique Payment Options

Unique weddings deserve unique payment options: We totally get that weddings come with a hefty price tag. That’s why we’re all about keeping things easy when you book us. We offer payment plans that suit your fancy. Whether you want to pay in monthly instalments, split it 50/50, or go with the 4-stage plan, the choice is yours. We’re here to make your wedding planning as stress-free as possible, including the payment part!

Friendly Support

Friendly support from start to finish: We’re all about professionalism and making your life easier. Once you’ve made your booking, we’ll send you a signed contract so you can have complete peace of mind. No need to worry about any unexpected curveballs! Our awesome management team will be right there with you until the big day, taking care of all the details. Trust us, it’s super simple and stress-free!

We firmly believe that great shows deserve great equipment.

That’s why we proudly offer an industry-standard PA system and lighting setup for all our bookings, at no additional charge. Our goal is to make the booking experience as smooth and hassle-free as possible, allowing you to focus on enjoying the event. With our top-of-the-line gear, your experience with us will be simply unmatched.
